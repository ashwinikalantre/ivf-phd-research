    Research Overview¶

    Broad Area¶

    Women’s healthcare, with focus on IVF outcome prediction and decision support.

    Student Context¶

    Item Details
    Department Computer Applications
    University Bharati Vidyapeeth, Pune
    Primary domain IVF
    Secondary interest Menopause
    Current focus AI, ML, XAI, CDSS and personalized IVF care

    Evidence Base¶

    • 35 papers are currently included.
    • Main period: 2021-2025.
    • One 2026 review is kept as background/synthesis only.
    • Papers include prediction studies, XAI papers, CDSS studies, embryo-selection papers, reviews, and one randomized trial.

    Main Research Direction¶

    The literature shows that IVF AI research is moving from simple prediction toward:

    • explainability
    • personalization
    • clinical decision support
    • external validation
    • real-world usability

    This supports a Computer Applications PhD topic because the problem needs data modeling, explainable AI, validation, and decision-support design.

    Current Working Topic¶

    An Explainable and Personalized Clinical Decision Support Framework for IVF Outcome Prediction Using Multimodal Clinical and Embryological Data

    Current Decision Point¶

    The next step is not more title writing.

    The next step is to confirm data feasibility with doctors and clinics.
