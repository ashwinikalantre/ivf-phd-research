    Literature Matrix¶

    This page is a readable site view of the working Excel matrix. The Excel workbook remains the source for detailed extraction fields.

    No Year Title Journal Objective Method Dataset Gap Found Relevance
    1 2025 Explainable artificial intelligence to identify follicles that optimize clinical outcomes during assisted conception Nature Communications Use XAI/ML to identify follicle-size ranges associated with mature oocytes and downstream IVF outcomes. Histogram-based gradient boosting regression tree; permutation importance; SHAP; logistic regression for live birth associations. 19,082 treatment-naive female patients from 11 European IVF centers. Need Prospective/RCT Validation; Need Clinical Decision Support; External/Indian Validation Gap High
    2 2024 Optimizing oocyte yield utilizing a machine learning model for dose and trigger decisions, a multi-center, prospective study Scientific Reports Evaluate clinical outcomes when clinicians use an AI platform for starting FSH dose and trigger-timing decisions. Commercial/clinical ML decision-support tools for starting dose and trigger timing. Prospective study with historical propensity-matched control; two US clinics, four physicians. Need Prospective/RCT Validation; Small Dataset; Limited Multi-center Validation; Need Real-world Deployment Evidence High
    3 2026 Multimodal intelligent prediction model for in vitro fertilization npj Digital Medicine Develop a multimodal IVF prediction model integrating embryo time-lapse imagery and clinical factors. Multimodal deep learning model for post-transfer outcome prediction. Chinese IVF data; day-3 transferred embryos only. Need External Validation; Limited Population Diversity; No Pre-transfer Recommendation; Retrospective Design High, but outside original 2021-2025 window
    4 2025 Deep learning classification integrating embryo images with associated clinical information from ART cycles Scientific Reports Test whether embryo-image models improve when combined with patient clinical information. Deep learning image model with clinical-data fusion. Embryo images and associated ART-cycle clinical data; transferred embryos only; limited database. Small Dataset; Selection Bias; Need Multimodal Data; No Pre-transfer Recommendation High
    5 2025 Machine learning center-specific models show improved IVF live birth predictions over US national registry-based model Nature Communications Compare center-specific ML live-birth prediction with a national registry-based model. Center-specific machine learning live-birth prediction models. 4,635 first-IVF-cycle patients from six centers. Limited Population Diversity; No Indian Population; Generalization Gap; Need External Validation High
    6 2025 Predictive modeling of pregnancy outcomes utilizing multiple machine learning techniques for IVF-ET BMC Pregnancy and Childbirth Identify factors influencing pregnancy outcomes and compare ML models for IVF-ET prediction. Multiple ML models; 80:20 train-test split. 2,625 women undergoing fresh IVF-ET cycles from 2016-2022 at Inner Mongolia Medical University affiliated hospital. Single-center Study; Need External Validation; Retrospective Design; Need Clinical Decision Support High
    7 2025 Construction and evaluation of machine learning-based prediction model for live birth following fresh embryo transfer in IVF/ICSI patients with PCOS Journal of Ovarian Research Predict live birth after fresh embryo transfer among PCOS patients using ML algorithms. Multiple ML algorithms; XGBoost best; SHAP for predictors. 1,062 fresh embryo transfer cycles in PCOS patients; 466 live births. Need External Validation; Disease-specific Generalization Gap; Need Clinical Decision Support Medium-High
    8 2025 Predictive models for live birth outcomes following fresh embryo transfer Journal of Translational Medicine Develop ML prediction models for live birth before embryo transfer. Machine learning prediction models. Fresh embryo-transfer cohort; exact size not extracted in first pass. Need External Validation; Need Clinical Decision Support; Need Real-world Deployment Evidence Medium-High
    9 2025 A retrospective analysis of 48,514 IVF cycles and an evaluation of machine learning models for live birth prediction Frontiers in Endocrinology Compare CNN and traditional ML models for live-birth prediction and assess feasibility in resource-limited settings. CNN, Random Forest and other traditional ML models. 48,514 IVF cycles from EMR data. Retrospective Design; Need External Validation; Need Real-world Deployment Evidence High
    10 2025 Optimizing predictive features using machine learning for early miscarriage risk following single vitrified-warmed blastocyst transfer Frontiers in Endocrinology Predict early miscarriage risk after single vitrified-warmed blastocyst transfer. Logistic Regression, Random Forest, Gradient Boosting, Voting Classifier. Dual-center retrospective analysis of 1,664 SVBT cycles, including 308 early miscarriage cases. Retrospective Design; Limited Multi-center Validation; Need Clinical Decision Support Medium
    11 2025 Machine learning-based prediction of IVF outcomes: the central role of female preprocedural factors Biomedicines Develop and validate per-cycle IVF success prediction using preprocedural clinical factors. XGBoost classifier using 14 baseline predictors and refined 9-variable model. 1,243 IVF/ICSI cycles from University of Szeged plus same-centre external validation cohort of 92 cycles. Need Multi-centre External Validation; Need Clinical Decision Support; Calibration Gap Medium-High
    12 2024 The prospect of artificial intelligence to personalize assisted reproductive technology npj Digital Medicine Review current and future AI implementations for personalized ART/IVF. Narrative review of AI applications, CDSS opportunities and translational barriers. Review article; no original dataset generated. Lack of Standardization; Need External Validation; Trustworthy AI Gap; Need Clinical Decision Support Very High for framing
    13 2025 Current progress and open challenges for applying artificial intelligence across the in vitro fertilization cycle Patterns Summarize AI-IVF applications by data modality and identify open challenges. Review/synthesis of structured clinical, image, video, omics and multimodal AI-IVF studies. Review article; no original dataset generated. Need External Validation; Trustworthy AI Gap; Need Multimodal Data; Need Real-world Deployment Evidence Very High for trend and gap synthesis
    14 2025 Patient-centred explainability in IVF outcome prediction arXiv preprint Evaluate understandability and trust in an IVF outcome prediction interface from patient feedback, survey and interviews. Human-centered XAI/interface evaluation. Four years of anonymous patient feedback plus user survey and interviews. Patient-centered XAI Gap; Trustworthy AI Gap; Need Personalized Explanation Medium-High
    15 2026 AI-based live birth prediction in IVF cycles: a systematic review without meta-analysis of model performance and validation Middle East Fertility Society Journal Systematically review AI-based live-birth prediction models in IVF cycles. Systematic review without meta-analysis. Systematic review of live-birth prediction studies; included because it directly synthesizes 2021-2025 evidence though published after target window. Retrospective Design; Selection Bias; Internal Validation Overfitting; Need External Validation High as synthesis, outside year window
    16 2024 Comparative study of machine learning approaches integrated with genetic algorithm for IVF success prediction PLOS ONE Compare ML algorithms with genetic-algorithm feature selection for IVF success prediction. ANN, RPART, Random Forest, SVM and AdaBoost with Genetic Algorithm feature selection. Single medical facility in Tehran, Iran; IVF clinical dataset. Single-center Study; Need External Validation; Limited Population Diversity; Need Clinical Decision Support High
    17 2024 Catalyzing IVF outcome prediction: exploring advanced machine learning paradigms for enhanced success rate prognostication Frontiers in Artificial Intelligence Improve IVF success-rate prediction through advanced ML and gynecological expertise. Logistic Regression, Gaussian NB, SVM, MLP, KNN, Random Forest, AdaBoost, LogitBoost, RUSBoost and RSM. Datasets from 2017-2018 and 2010-2016. Retrospective Design; Need Prospective/RCT Validation; Need Clinical Decision Support High
    18 2022 Comparison of machine learning classification techniques to predict implantation success in an IVF treatment cycle Reproductive BioMedicine Online Compare ML models for predicting implantation success in IVF cycles and variable importance. Machine learning classification techniques. IVF treatment-cycle dataset; full dataset details require paper verification. Need Full-text Verification; Need External Validation Medium
    19 2022 Predicting clinical pregnancy using clinical features and machine learning algorithms in in vitro fertilization PLOS ONE Construct ML models for clinical pregnancy prediction in IVF using clinical features. Random Forest and Logistic Regression. IVF patients from 2007-2019. Need Full-text Verification; Need External Validation; Need Clinical Decision Support Medium-High
    20 2022 Machine Learning Approach to Predict Clinical Pregnancy Potential in Women Undergoing IVF Program Fertility & Reproduction Use ML data mining to construct a clinical pregnancy prediction model for IVF. Decision Tree and Random Forest. Large practical IVF clinical database; exact size/source require verification. Lack of Standardization; Need External Validation; Need Clinical Decision Support High for gap argument
    21 2025 Artificial intelligence-driven precision treatment of reproductive medicine-related diseases: the optimal protocol choice for IVF-ET Journal of Advanced Research Develop an AI system for personalized ovarian stimulation protocol choice in IVF-ET. Adaptive ensemble/ACA-FI framework using RF, XGBoost and SOIL feature-selection approaches. 17,791 patients undergoing OS and IVF/ICSI at Tongji Hospital, May 2015-May 2019. Single-center Study; Need External Validation; Need Clinical Decision Support; Personalized Recommendation Gap Very High
    22 2025 Real-world use of an artificial intelligence-powered clinical decision support tool for ovarian stimulation F&S Reports Evaluate real-world use of Stim Assist CDSS for FSH dosing and trigger timing during ovarian stimulation. AI-powered CDSS with Starting Dose and Trigger algorithms. Single IVF clinic real-world deployment cohort; exact size requires full-paper verification. Single-center Study; Need Prospective/RCT Validation; Need Real-world Deployment Evidence; Need External Validation Very High
    23 2024 A new decision-support tool in a multi-center randomized trial for personalized, optimized, and simplified fertility treatment in non-PCOS patients Reproduction and Fertility Evaluate Opt-IVF CDSS for personalized gonadotropin dosing and reduced ultrasound monitoring in non-PCOS IVF patients. Physics-based model and optimal control theory decision-support tool. Multi-center randomized trial with 115 women aged 25-45; 55 intervention and 60 control. Small Dataset; Limited Population Scope; Need External Validation; Need Clinical Decision Support High
    24 2023 Towards Automation in IVF: Pre-Clinical Validation of a Deep Learning-Based Embryo Grading System during PGT-A Cycles Journal of Clinical Medicine Validate a deep-learning embryo grading/ranking system during PGT-A cycles. iDAScore v1.0 deep learning model using 3D CNN. Retrospective pre-clinical external validation including 3,604 blastocysts and 808 euploid transfers from 1,232 cycles. Retrospective Design; Need External Validation; Need Prospective/RCT Validation; Black-box/Explainability Gap High
    25 2025 An explainable ultrasound-based machine learning model for predicting reproductive outcomes after frozen embryo transfer Reproductive BioMedicine Online Develop an explainable ultrasound radiomics plus clinical model for reproductive outcome prediction after FET. Radiomics model, clinical logistic regression model, XGBoost fusion model, SHAP explanations. Prospective study of 787 infertile females undergoing FET; train n=550, test n=237. Single-center Study; Need External Validation; Need Multimodal Data; Manual/Subjective Workflow Bias Very High
    26 2021 Interpretable, not black-box, artificial intelligence should be used for embryo selection Human Reproduction Open Argue for interpretable AI and rigorous evaluation before embryo-selection AI is ethically implemented. Ethical and methodological critique. Commentary/review of embryo-selection AI literature. Black-box/Explainability Gap; Trustworthy AI Gap; Need Prospective/RCT Validation; Patient-centered XAI Gap Very High for conceptual framing
    27 2024 Beyond black-box models: explainable AI for embryo ploidy prediction and patient-centric consultation Journal of Assisted Reproduction and Genetics Use XAI to improve accuracy and transparency for embryo ploidy prediction using embryonic and clinical data. RF, LDA, LR, SVM, AdaBoost and LightGBM with SHAP and LIME. 1,908 blastocyst embryos with ploidy status, morphokinetic features, morphology grades and 11 clinical variables. Black-box/Explainability Gap; Patient-centered XAI Gap; Need External Validation High
    28 2024 Deep learning versus manual morphology-based embryo selection in IVF: a randomized, double-blind noninferiority trial Nature Medicine Assess whether deep learning embryo selection is noninferior to standard morphology-based embryo selection. Deep learning embryo selection using iDAScore compared with embryologist morphology-based selection. Multicenter randomized double-blind noninferiority trial across 14 IVF clinics in Australia and Europe; 1,066 patients, 533 per arm. Need Prospective/RCT Validation; Need Real-world Deployment Evidence; Trustworthy AI Gap; Clinical Utility Gap Very High
    29 2025 Artificial intelligence-enhanced in-vitro fertilization outcome prediction for the Indian subpopulation: integrating pre-treatment parameters and Bayesian-optimized ensemble techniques International Journal of Community Medicine and Public Health Develop and evaluate an ML model for estimating cumulative live-birth probability in the first complete IVF cycle among Indian subpopulations. Bayesian-optimized ensemble model, reported as BoVe. Retrospective cohort using IVF-ICSI data from Sir Ganga Ram Hospital, Delhi, India, from 1 January 2018 to 31 October 2022; original dataset 2,908 records and 79 variables. No Indian Population; Single-center Study; Need External Validation; Retrospective Design; Need Clinical Decision Support High for Indian validation angle
    30 2024 Machine learning algorithms in constructing prediction models for assisted reproductive technology related live birth outcomes Scientific Reports Construct and compare prediction models for ART-related live birth outcomes using clinical indicators. Statistical models and multiple ML algorithms; logistic regression recommended for simplicity in source summary. Large Chinese ART patient sample; exact cohort size requires full paper extraction. Need Full-text Verification; Need External Validation; Lack of Standardization; Need Clinical Decision Support Medium-High
    31 2025 Machine learning prediction of clinical pregnancy in endometriosis patients undergoing fresh embryo transfer BMC/PMC indexed article Develop an ML-based predictive model for clinical pregnancy in endometriosis patients undergoing fresh embryo transfer. Naive Bayes, Logistic Regression, Random Forest, KNN, Neural Network and XGBoost with feature selection and cross-validation. 1,752 endometriosis patients undergoing IVF/ICSI with fresh embryo transfer from 2014 to 2024. Disease-specific Generalization Gap; Retrospective Design; Need External Validation; Need Clinical Decision Support High for personalization/subgroup theme
    32 2025 Artificial intelligence in assisted reproductive technology: separating the dream from reality Reproductive BioMedicine Online Critically review the evidence for AI in ART and separate realistic clinical value from overclaiming. Critical narrative review. Critical review of AI applications in ART/IVF. Need External Validation; Clinical Utility Gap; Trustworthy AI Gap; Need Real-world Deployment Evidence High for thesis justification
    33 2025 Development of an explainable machine learning model to predict live birth versus miscarriage among IVF-ET pregnancies Journal of Medical Artificial Intelligence Develop an interpretable ML model for live birth versus miscarriage prediction among patients with confirmed pregnancy after IVF-ET. XGBoost explainable model with interpretable tree/feature analysis. IVF-ET pregnancies with confirmed pregnancy; exact cohort details require full extraction. Need External Validation; Need Clinical Decision Support; Trustworthy AI Gap; Need Full-text Verification Medium-High
    34 2025 A review of artificial intelligence applications in in vitro fertilization Journal of Assisted Reproduction and Genetics Review recent AI applications across IVF, including embryo, gamete and clinical decision areas. Narrative review. Review paper. Need External Validation; Need Real-world Deployment Evidence; Lack of Standardization; Need Clinical Decision Support High for background and trend analysis
    35 2025 Global trends in the use of artificial intelligence in reproductive medicine: insights from surveys of international fertility specialists Journal of IVF-Worldwide Assess global AI adoption and attitudes in reproductive medicine using surveys of fertility specialists. Survey analysis. Survey data from 383 eligible respondents in 2022 and 171 primarily fertility-specialist respondents in 2025. Need Real-world Deployment Evidence; Trustworthy AI Gap; Clinical Utility Gap; Need Clinician Usability Evaluation Medium-High for deployment context
