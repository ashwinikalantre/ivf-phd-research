    Candidate Expansion Queue¶

    These papers look useful for expanding the matrix from 27 papers toward 35-40 strong papers. They should not be treated as extracted evidence until the abstract, conclusion, limitations and future work are checked.

    Priority Candidate Paper Why It May Be Useful Action
    High Illingworth et al., 2024, Deep learning versus manual morphology-based embryo selection in IVF: a randomized, double-blind noninferiority trial, Nature Medicine Strong prospective/RCT evidence for AI embryo selection; helps balance many retrospective studies. Add full extraction.
    High Artificial Intelligence in Routine IVF Practice, 2026 review Useful synthesis of routine IVF deployment and external validation evidence, but outside target window. Use as synthesis/background only.
    High Current progress and open challenges for applying artificial intelligence in IVF, 2025, Patterns Useful for open challenges, modalities and validation gaps. Add as review evidence if full text confirms gaps.
    High Machine learning algorithms in constructing prediction models for clinical outcomes after IVF/ICSI-ET in patients with endometriosis, 2024, Scientific Reports Disease-specific IVF outcome prediction; likely useful for subgroup/personalization theme. Extract dataset, features and limitations.
    Medium Bioinformatic Analysis of Complex In Vitro Fertilization Data and Predictive Model Design Based on Machine Learning, 2025, Biology May support live-birth prediction and complex IVF data integration. Verify limitations and relevance.
    Medium Development of an explainable machine learning model to predict IVF-ET outcomes in patients with confirmed pregnancy, 2025 Supports XAI and external-validation gap. Verify journal quality and exact cohort.
    Medium Artificial intelligence-enhanced in-vitro fertilization outcome prediction in Indian subpopulations, 2025 Potentially important for Indian population angle. Verify peer-review status, methods and data source before using.
    Medium Evaluation of Artificial Intelligence for Embryo Selection During IVF: A Prospective, Randomized Controlled Trial, 2025 Potentially useful RCT/deployment evidence. Verify publication status and source reliability.

    Rule for Adding to Matrix¶

    Move a candidate into the Excel matrix only when at least these fields are known:

    • title
    • authors
    • year
    • journal
    • objective
    • dataset
    • method/algorithm
    • evaluation metrics
    • limitations
    • source URL or DOI
