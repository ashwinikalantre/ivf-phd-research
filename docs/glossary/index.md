# Study Glossary

This glossary explains important words that appear in IVF-AI papers.

It is not a medical textbook. It is a study aid for reading literature-review papers confidently.

## Why We Need This

In IVF-AI papers, many terms look like simple words but carry clinical or technical meaning.

Examples:

- follicle
- oocyte
- blastocyst
- trigger day
- SHAP
- cross-validation
- calibration

If we do not understand these terms, the paper summary may look correct but the research meaning may be weak.

## How To Use

When a paper note uses an important term, we will add it to the relevant glossary.

Each entry should include:

1. simple meaning
2. why it matters in IVF-AI research
3. how it connects to our topic
4. external link for deeper study

## Rule

Definitions should be simple but not careless.

For clinical terms, prefer sources such as HFEA, CDC ART, ASRM or peer-reviewed glossary documents.

For technical terms, prefer official documentation or standard academic sources.
