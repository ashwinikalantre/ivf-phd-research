## IVF Success Rates

Success rates depend on the denominator.

| Denominator | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Per cycle started | Includes all started cycles, even if no transfer happens. |
| Per aspiration/egg retrieval | Based on cycles where eggs were retrieved. |
| Per embryo transfer | Based only on cycles where embryo transfer happened. |
| Cumulative live birth | Includes fresh and frozen transfers from one retrieval or multiple cycles. |

## Presentation Caution

Do not mix success rates from different countries or denominators as if they are the same.

Always say:

> This number depends on age group, outcome definition, and denominator.
